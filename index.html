'A Beautiful DOGGY :)'

Their sense of smell is at least 40x better than ours.

  • SCIENTIFIC NAME: Canis lupus familiaris
  • AVERAGE HEIGHT: 15 – 110 cm
  • AVERAGE LIFESPAN: 10-13 years
  • HABITAT: forests and brush lands

A dog has sharp teeth so that it can eat flesh very easily, it has four legs, two ears, two eyes, a tail, a mouth, and a nose.

It is a very clever animal and is very useful in catching thieves. It runs very fast, barks loudly and attacks the strangers.

A dog saves the life of the master from danger.
